Sweat Hut Fitness and Sporting Club

Project Brief:

The project was a university assignment which looked at creating an online presence for a company and programming some authentication.

The website was developed in HTML5 and CSS3 and the login back and validation was coded in PHP. I also used a combination of Javascript and PHP to validate the form fields on the contact page.

This was a good assignment to understand the best practise of form validation and to get an understanding of the types security aspects you need to consider when working with PHP and forms.
