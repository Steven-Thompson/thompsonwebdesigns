Clovelly Electrical and Communications

Project Brief:

This project was for an electrical company which wanted to showcase some of its work and services online and through Facebook. The company's requirements were simple. They wanted to keep the website basic and use it as a platform to showcase installations and advertise services.

One key point I took away from this project was how import Search Engine Optimisation (SEO) has become for company's which rely on online business. I conducted some research and implemented SEO best practise techniques to the website.

I also set-up Google Analytics which helped to company track their online performance and assists in the SEO process.
