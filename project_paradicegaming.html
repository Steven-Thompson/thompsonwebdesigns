Project Brief:

Paradice Gaming is an e-commerce website that sells board games which is powered by wordpress. Paradice Gaming was a university assignment which involved the creation of a company and its visual identity. The goal of the assignment was to learn to work with Content Management Systems and to build a companyâ€™s online presence from the ground up.

I developed a custom theme for wordpress and used a plugin called woocommerce to handle the products and checkout system.

This was a great project to undertake to give me a greater understanding of content management systems and the role they can play in e-commerce websites.

Custom theme developed (Underscores based theme)
